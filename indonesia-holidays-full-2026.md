## Notes

- Cuti bersama are additional leave days declared by the government to create longer weekends. They are not public holidays per se but are often granted to civil servants and many private companies follow.
- The dates are official based on the 2025 SKB; they may be adjusted by subsequent regulations, but this is the expected calendar.
- For precise day-of-week calculations, the table above includes the day name.
