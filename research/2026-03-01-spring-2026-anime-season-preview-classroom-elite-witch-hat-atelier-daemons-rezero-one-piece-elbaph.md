## Context: Why Spring 2026 Feels Different

The unusual density of this season is partly structural. Several major franchises that originally targeted 2025 were pushed into 2026 due to production delays (COVID-era backlog finally clearing), and an unusually large number of manga series concluded or reached climactic arcs simultaneously. The result is a Spring 2026 that resembles Fall 2023 (Frieren, Undead Unluck, Pluto all premiering simultaneously) in its concentration of prestige content.

The **Daemons + Witch Hat Atelier** pairing is particularly notable: two new adaptations of acclaimed, long-running manga debuting in the same week (April 4â€“6), both with exceptional production pedigrees. This is the kind of coincidence that creates "golden season" narratives in retrospect.
