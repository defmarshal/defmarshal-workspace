### 14 Macro-Trends: The Key Ones

**Agentic commerce** is the sharpest inflection point. AI agents are making real purchases online ‚Äî not recommending, *buying*. Visa and Mastercard are actively building bot payment verification protocols. The liability question (who's responsible when an agent buys the wrong thing?) is unresolved and will define policy in 2026‚Äì2027.

**Open finance** extends beyond banking to payroll, pensions, tax, insurance. India's Account Aggregator processed **1.2 billion data requests in 2024** ‚Äî the largest open finance deployment globally, and a proof of scale for what the Philippines and Malaysia are building now with their own open-finance roadmaps.

**Real-time payments as core infrastructure** ‚Äî PromptPay (Thailand), UPI (India), FedNow (US), PayNow (Singapore), InstaPay (Philippines). These aren't fintech features anymore; they're the settlement layer everything else builds on. Millisecond fraud detection, event-based pricing, and liquidity automation are only possible because the rails are now instant.

**CBDCs and tokenised RWAs** are going live. Thailand is piloting mBridge (cross-border), Singapore's MAS Project Guardian is tokenising bonds and FX, and the EU Digital Euro is in the European Parliament. Tokenised real-world assets are creating entirely new liquidity pools for embedded finance products.

**Deobanks** ‚Äî decentralised + neobank hybrids. On-chain transparency, smart contracts, compliance-ready design, token rewards. Early stage but attracting serious developer attention in SEA crypto-native demographics.

### The SEA Angle

Southeast Asia is the global benchmark for embedded finance:

- **290M+ unbanked adults** ‚Äî massive underserved population with no incumbent to displace
- **Super-app distribution already built** ‚Äî Grab, GoTo/Gojek, Sea/Shopee have solved the last-mile problem
- **Regulatory sandbox experiments** ‚Äî SG, TH, ID, MY all running BaaS pilot frameworks
- **$130B+ annual remittance flows** ‚Äî massive target for cross-border real-time rails

Full table of players: GrabFinance, SeaBank (90M+ Shopee users), Tonik (first PH neobank to go BaaS), Akulaku, Kredivo, 2C2P, TrueMoney (26M+ users), Momo (31M+ Vietnam).
