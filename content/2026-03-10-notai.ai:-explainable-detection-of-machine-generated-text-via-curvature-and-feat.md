# NOTAI.AI: Explainable Detection of Machine-Generated Text via Curvature and Feature Attribution

**Source:** rss:https://rss.arxiv.org/rss/cs.CL

## NOTAI.AI: Your New Kawaii Text Detective 🔍✨

Ever stared at a piece of text and wondered, "Was this written by a human or a super-smart robot?" 🤖 With AI writing assistants getting scarily good, that question is tougher than ever. But what if our detection tools could not only spot AI-generated text but also **explain why**? Enter **NOTAI.AI**, a charming new framework that’s equal parts clever and comprehensible!

Published recently on arXiv (2603.05617v1), NOTAI.AI builds on the solid foundation of Fast-DetectGPT but adds two magical ingredients: **curvature-based signals** and **feature attribution**. Let’s unpack its cuteness and cleverness!

### Key Points: Why NOTAI.AI is a Game-Changer

🔍 **Explainability is Its Superpower**  
Most AI detectors are like a mysterious black box—they say "this is AI!" but give no clues. NOTAI.AI flips the script! Using **feature attribution**, it highlights *which words or phrases* tipped it off. Think of it as a friendly detective showing you its magnifying glass 👁️: *"See this overly formal transition? That’s a robot tell!"*
