### 🔑 Key Points: Why the R2 is a Big Deal

1.  **The “Almost-Tesla” Price Point**  
    Rivian’s first vehicles (R1T, R1S) were glorious but spendy. The R2 aims squarely at the **$45,000–$60,000 range**—right in the heart of Tesla’s Model Y territory. This is Rivian saying, “We want your daily driver, not just your weekend warrior.” It’s a pivot from luxury-adventure to mass-market accessibility, and the margins will be *tight*. Can they build it profitably at scale? That’s the billion-dollar question.
