## Introduction: ChatGPT Leaves the Chat Window

OpenAI has transformed ChatGPT from a conversational AI into a **productivity hub** by launching app integrations. Now you can play a Spotify playlist, book a ride on Uber, order dinner via DoorDash, design in Canva, book travel on Expedia, or mock up a UI in Figma—all without leaving the chat. These integrations are built on **OpenAI’s new “actions” framework**, which lets ChatGPT securely connect to third‑party services via OAuth and execute tasks based on natural‑language instructions. No more copy-pasting links or switching tabs—just tell ChatGPT what you want, and it will handle the rest.

Here’s how to get started and make the most of these integrations.

---

## Setting Up Integrations: One-Time Connections

Before you can command ChatGPT to play music or book a flight, you need to **link your accounts**:

1. Open ChatGPT (desktop or mobile).
2. Click your profile → **Settings** → **Integrations** (or look for the “Connect apps” banner).
3. Browse the available apps and click **Connect**.
4. You’ll be redirected to the app’s OAuth screen—log in and authorize the requested permissions (e.g., “play music,” “view your playlists,” “create events”).
5. Once connected, the app appears in your ChatGPT sidebar with a green checkmark.

**Important:** You can revoke access anytime from the same settings page. ChatGPT only acts on your explicit instructions; it won’t perform actions without your command in the current session.

## Key Integrations and How to Use Them

### 🎵 Spotify – Music by Voice

Once connected, you can control Spotify playback and playlists:

* “ChatGPT, play lo‑fi beats on Spotify.”
* “Add this song to my ‘Road Trip’ playlist.”
* “What’s the top track from The Weeknd?”
* “Pause the music.”

ChatGPT will open Spotify (or your active device) and execute the command. It can also search for songs, artists, or albums and queue them up. Great for hands‑free music control while you work.

---

### 🎨 Canva & Figma – Design Without Switching Apps

For designers and non‑designers alike, these integrations let you create visuals from within ChatGPT:

* **Canva:** “Create a social media banner for my coffee shop, 1200×628 pixels, with a warm theme.” ChatGPT will open Canva, generate a draft using your brand kit (if connected), and return a shareable link.
* **Figma:** “Make a wireframe for a mobile login screen with email and password fields.” ChatGPT will create a new Figma file with basic shapes and layers, ready for refinement.

You can iterate: “Add a logo to the top left” or “Change the primary color to blue.” This is a game‑changer for rapid prototyping.

---

### ✈️ Expedia – Travel Planning Made Conversational

Booking flights, hotels, and activities becomes a dialogue:

* “Find me a round‑trip flight from Bangkok to Tokyo next week, $500 max.”
* “Book a hotel in London near Covent Garden for March 10–12.”
* “What are some kid‑friendly activities in Singapore this weekend?”

ChatGPT will pull live data from Expedia (if you’ve linked your account) and present options. You can then say “Book that flight” or “Reserve the third hotel” and ChatGPT will complete the transaction using saved payment methods (you’ll be prompted to confirm).

## Best Practices for Using Integrations

1. **Be specific**: Include details like time, quantity, preferences. “Play rock music” is vague; “play Led Zeppelin on Spotify” works better.
2. **Use context**: ChatGPT remembers your chat history, so you can say “Book the same hotel as last time” if you’ve previously booked.
3. **Verify before executing**: For expensive or irreversible actions (flights, large purchases), ChatGPT will ask for confirmation. Always review the summary.
4. **Manage permissions**: Regularly check which apps are connected and revoke any you no longer use.
5. **Combine integrations**: You can chain actions—e.g., “Find a vegan restaurant on OpenTable, book a table, and add it to my Google Calendar.” ChatGPT will coordinate across services.

---

## Limitations and Caveats

- **Not all actions are supported**: Integrations typically cover common workflows but may not expose every API feature (e.g., Spotify can’t create collaborative playlists yet).
- **Regional availability**: Some apps (like DoorDash, Uber) are US‑centric; international users may have limited options.
- **Authentication expiry**: OAuth tokens may need refreshing every few months; you’ll be prompted to reconnect.
- **Privacy**: ChatGPT can see the data you grant access to. Only connect apps you trust and review permissions carefully.
