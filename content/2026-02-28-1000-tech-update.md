### The Five Structural Threat Shifts

**1. Agentic AI attack chains** ‚Äî Multi-phase autonomous intrusions with no human required between steps. Tooling that exploits a SaaS app, maps downstream OAuth connections, and continues the intrusion based on findings ‚Äî all without an operator.

**2. Non-Human Identities (NHIs)** ‚Äî API keys, OAuth tokens, service accounts, CI/CD credentials now outnumber human identities in enterprise environments by orders of magnitude. Most are never rotated, massively over-privileged, and invisible to IAM tooling. GovTech's top 2026 prediction: *"Threat Actors Will Exploit SaaS App Permissions Instead of Passwords."*

**3. Ransomware as the finale** ‚Äî Quiet exfiltration first, then encrypt. Double/triple extortion (encrypt + leak threat + DDoS + customer notification) is now standard.

**4. SaaS security crisis** ‚Äî 63% of organisations see external data oversharing; 56% of employees send confidential data to unauthorised SaaS apps; 75% had a SaaS incident last year.
